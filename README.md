## 🚀 Innovation

WellPredict is a horizontal platform technology delivering privacy-preserving predictive analytics for psychosocial risk detection. Using cryptographic aggregation and differential privacy, we enable organisations to identify workforce crises before they occur—without compromising individual privacy.

**Patent Status**: UK IPO Application No. **GB2603869.5** filed 22 Feb 2026  
*System and Method for Privacy-Preserving Prediction of Psychosocial Risk Using Cryptographic Aggregation*

## 🏗️ Architecture

### Core Engine (Universal)
- **Encrypted 90-second check-ins** - Minimal friction data capture
- **Cryptographic aggregation** - Homomorphic encryption ensures individual data remains mathematically unidentifiable
- **Prediction algorithms** - 3-4 week advance warning for adult workforces, 1-2 weeks for student populations
- **Risk scoring engine** - Normalised 0-100 scale with team/department-level granularity (minimum 8 people)

## 🔒 Privacy & Compliance

- **GDPR Article 9 Compliant** - Special category data handling by design
- **UK Data Residency** - AWS UK regions only
- **Cryptographic Separation** - Individual identity separated from point of capture
- **No Surveillance** - Unlike Microsoft Viva or similar tools, we never read emails or individual communications
- **Minimum Group Size** - 8+ people required for any aggregate reporting
